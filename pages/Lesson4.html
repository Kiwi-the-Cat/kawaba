Lesson 4 - Grammar

This lesson aims to cover the rest of kawaba grammar.

As covered in Lesson 2, the basic structure of a kawaba sentence is as follows:

{context |context} {na |context marker} {location |location} {ni | location marker} {time |time} {nu | temporal marker} {subject |subject} {ne | subject marker} {verb |verb} {no | object marker} {object |object}
